Obanai Iguro

Obanai Iguro (伊黒小芭内) is a major character in the 2016-2020 dark fantasy manga series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and its 2019 anime adaptation. Obanai was a Demon Slayer and the Serpent Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, standing amongst the mighty Hashira. He is rather infamous in the slayer corps for his cynical behavior, often criticizing the new members of the corps being weak. He also harbors feelings for the Love Hashira Mitsuri Kanroji, and gets easily jealous on those who get too close to her. He is voiced by Keichi Suzumura in Japanese and by Erik Scott Krimerer in English.
