Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz is a trademark and the company of the same name - a manufacturer of premium passenger cars, trucks, buses and other vehicles, part of the German concern " Mercedes-Benz Group ". It is one of the most recognizable automobile brands worldwide [ 5 ] . Mercedes-Benz headquarters are located in Stuttgart , Baden-WÃ¼rttemberg , Germany . The brand name was approved on June 28, 1926 [ 6 ] as a result of the merger of two competing firms, Benz & Cie. (founded by Karl Benz ) and Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft (founded by Gottlieb Daimler ), into a single concern - Daimler-Benz . The brand name is derived from two of the most significant cars of the combined companies - the Mercedes of 1901 and the Benz Patent-Motorwagen of 1886. In 2018, the Mercedes-Benz brand was valued at $ 48.601 billion , ranking second (after Toyota ) among automobile manufacturers and eighth among all brands in the world [ 5 ] . According to BrandZ , in 2018, the brand was included in the Top 100 Most Valuable Global Brands list , where it ranked 46th among the most valuable brands with a value of $25.684 billion [ 7 ] . In 2019, the Mercedes-Benz brand was valued at $60.355 billion, thereby ranking first in the ranking of automobile manufacturers [ 8 ] . In January 2023, the Mercedes-Benz brand was ranked second among auto brands by Brand Finance , valued at $ 58.8 billion , behind Tesla [ 9 ] . In February 2024, the Mercedes-Benz brand took first place among all automobile brands. Brand Finance analysts valued the Mercedes-Benz brand at $59.4 billion, in the overall list of all brands in the world, Mercedes-Benz took 17th place [ 10 ] .
