Mercedes-Benz (German pronunciation: [mɛʁˌtse:dəs 'bɛnts, -dɛs -] ⓘ),[4][5] commonly referred to simply as Mercedes and occasionally as Benz, is a German luxury and commercial vehicle brand that was founded in 1926.

Mercedes-Benz Group

Mercedes-Benz Group established in 2019 is based in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany.[1]
